Hello there! We're Oranje Chess.

We love teaching kids chess in Queensland.


Chess is incredibly good for children of all ages!

Chess playing kids are actually learning kids!
Chess teaches dozens of abilities, both mental, social and behavioural.
Chess is free, chess is fun and chess is teaching kids every moment they play.

We believe, along with educators around the world, that chess is an invaluable instrument of education. See our portfolio page for resources and research on how chess can help your child and school.
